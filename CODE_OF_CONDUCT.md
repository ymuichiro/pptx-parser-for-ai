# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Provide actionable feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or doxxing
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Report incidents to maintainers via `security@ymuichiro.dev`. Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or ban contributors for serious or repeated violations.
