AI Governance Readiness

Move from local experimentation to enterprise-grade control

A production-ready deck should feel restrained, dense enough for executive review, and structured around clear decisions rather than decoration.

Current pattern

Teams run isolated pilots with uneven review depth and limited reuse of controls.

  • Policy interpretation varies by business unit
  • Evidence collection happens late in delivery
  • Model and data reviews are often manual

Target pattern

A repeatable intake, review, and evidence model supports faster scaled deployment.

  • One intake standard for risk classification
  • Control templates embedded in product delivery
  • Continuous evidence capture for audit readiness
